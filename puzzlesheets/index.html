Puzzle Sheets Add-on for Google Sheets™

Puzzle Sheets is a Google Sheets™ add-on for generating Google spreadsheets designed to assist with solving a variety of puzzle types.

Currently, the following puzzle types are supported.

  • Crosswords (both blocked and barred, normal and cryptic)
  • Acrostics

Privacy Policy

Puzzle Sheets does not record nor use your personal information in any way, shape, or form. No data is sent to us at all when you use it, and we have no knowledge of who is using the add-on.

Puzzle Sheets uses the following sensitive permissions:

  1. https://www.googleapis.com/auth/script.container.ui is necessary to show the modal dialogs used to customize the puzzle sheets.
  2. https://www.googleapis.com/auth/spreadsheets is necessary to use Batch Update to create and update spreadsheets.

Puzzle Sheets use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
